Google O-Auth:

My teams purpose was to understand the common use of open authorization (OAuth) as a means for enabling internet users to access and use secure third party websites or services using their Microsoft, Google, Facebook or Twitter accounts without exposing their passwords. The scope of stakeholder interests in OAuth was explored, with particular focus on the data security and privacy interests of end users. We focused on how behavioral economic principles and strategies could encouraging normative values and principled use of OAuth in practice. This effort was sponsored by GoogleUX.
